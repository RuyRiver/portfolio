Vibra was built for Fascaster to revolutionize the user experience on Farcaster, an open social protocol that was growing monotonous with its Twitter-like feed. We embraced the challenge to turn it vertical and engaging, creating a refreshing, TikTok-inspired scrolling experience that energizes users.

Audience & Inspiration

Farcaster's community—composed of blockchain builders, founders, artists, and enthusiasts—expressed a unanimous sentiment: "Social-Fi is boring." With a primary audience of rebellious millennial nerds in the USA, we tapped into cultural nostalgia from a beloved classic music TV channel. This inspiration birthed a disruptive brand identity: bold, wild, and a true celebration of creativity.

Design & Interface

We designed the interface with echoes of old-school operating systems, overlaid with modern brutalist aesthetics. The result is a visual experience that is both nostalgic and cutting-edge—resonating deeply with users who appreciate both form and function.

Innovative Integrations

Vertical Video Feed

Inspired by TikTok's engaging scroll to keep users entertained.

Fan Tokens

A novel De-Fi feature assigning each creator a unique token for trading and community support.

Integrated Crypto Wallet

Manages both standard tokens and Fan Tokens seamlessly within the same interface.

Live Streaming

Adds a dynamic, real-time layer to the social experience, blending text-based feeds with interactive broadcasts.
